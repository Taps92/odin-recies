Rice and chicken

List of ingridients

  • Cooking oil
  • 2l of water
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Cicken
  • Salt
  • Raw rice
  • Chicken spice
  • Carrots

Steps to make Rice and Chicken

  1. Take 3 cups of water and add 1 teaspoon of cooking oil
  2. Let the mixture boil for 2 mins and add 1 cup of Rice, let it boil for 30 minutes
  3. Cut the chicken into pieces and marinate the chicken with chicken spice and salt
  4. Put 3 tablespoons of cooking oil in a frying pan and let it boil for 3 minutes
  5. Put the marinated chicken into the pan and fry it for 5 minutes
  6. Cut tomatoes,onion and tomatoes fry them to make soup
  7. Put the Rice in the plate and add the fresh carrots
  8. Add the soup and Chicken into the plate and serve the delicious dish
