Chips

List of ingridients

  • Cooking oil
  • Potatoes
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Salt

The steps to make Chips

  1. Peal the potatoes and cut them
  2. Put 1 litre of cooking oil in the frying pan and let it boil for a minute
  3. Deep the potatoes in the pan until they are a bit dry
  4. Cut onions and tomatoes
  5. Serve the chips with tomatoes,onions and salt
