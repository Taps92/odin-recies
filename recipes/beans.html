Beans

List of ingridients

  • Cooking oil
  • 2l of water
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Galic
  • Salt

The steps to make Beans

  1. Put 1 cup of beans in the pot with 3 cups of water
  2. Add 1 table spoon of water and keep on adding water until 2 hours
  3. Put the beans in bolw and put one tablespoon of cooking oil in the pot
  4. Cut tomatoes and onions and put them in the hot to make soup
  5. On that soup add reasonable salt and put 1 galic
  6. Leave it on the stove for 5 minutes and you are ready to enjoy
